Getting Started

  1. Download and install VoiceType for your operating system
  2. Launch VoiceType — it will appear in your system tray / menu bar
  3. Sign in with your LinkBoard account (same email/password)
  4. Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Space to start recording, release to transcribe
  5. Text is automatically typed into whatever app has focus
