Overview

LAS LinkBoard ("the Service") is a tool for Leysin American School staff to create dynamic QR codes and track email campaign opens. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Google account email and name — used to authenticate you via Google Sign-In and associate your campaigns with your account.
  • Email campaign metadata — subject lines you enter and recipient lists you create for open-tracking campaigns.
  • Email open events — when a tracked email is opened, we log the timestamp. We do not collect the recipient's IP address, location, or device information.
  • QR code links — the URLs you save to generate dynamic QR codes.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display your campaign analytics (open counts, timestamps).
  • To power dynamic QR code redirects.
  • To authenticate and secure your account.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to data handled by Google.
  • Supabase — our database provider. Data is stored securely in Supabase-managed infrastructure.
  • Vercel — hosts the application and serverless API functions.

Chrome Extension Permissions

The LAS LinkBoard Tracker extension requests these permissions:

  • activeTab — to inject the tracking toggle into Gmail's compose window.
  • storage — to save your authentication state locally.
  • identity — to sign in with your Google account.

The extension only activates on mail.google.com and does not access any other websites.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact the school IT department or the extension developer.

Children's Privacy

This service is intended for school staff use only and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date below.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the Leysin American School IT department.
